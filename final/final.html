Overview of Universal Design

Universal design is the practice of creating something with accessibility in mind. It involves starting off incorporating accessible practices from the beginning, as opposed to accessible design, which brings accessibility to pre-existing entities. Let's take a look at WHY universal design is important, WHAT it is and involves, and HOW to incorporate universal design into your work.
